Design a parking system

This project is to design a interface of parking system based on the situation that difficults to find a parking in mall. In this system the main function is to traking our car and navigate us to empty parking slot. There have three color to separate the situation of parking slot, red show the slot is already have car, the yellow color is have car is going to the parking slot and green color is a empty parking slot. There also have a time traking to know the parking time. The online payment also available in this system. Besides that, there also have general feature like resigter an account, customer servies and point reward.


This is a group assignment, part involed are the overall color, font, logo and animation. The interface of tracking to empty parking slot, payment, side menu, point, FAQs, Feedback and thankyou, Settings interface.
