Migration to Front-End

The migration of back-end development to front-end development focused on addressing challenges related to adjusting tracking zone lines. My primary objective was to successfully retrieve values from JSON data. This involved integrating a canvas element and dynamically drawing multiple lines based on the provided data. Each line was differentiated by color, x, and y coordinates to ensuring clear visualization and functionality. This task allowed me to leverage front-end technologies effectively to enhance the tracking zone line adjustments previously handled by the back-end.

By addressing these aspects, I aimed to enhance the overall quality and usability of each website, making them more engaging and user-friendly for visitors.
